About Nuit Noire

A live, city-aware night safety platform for Mumbai that blends geospatial data, routing intelligence, and community reporting.

What Nuit Noire Does

Nuit Noire is a location-first safety system for late-evening and night travel in Mumbai. It helps people assess neighborhood risk, plan safer routes, and understand what is driving risk in each area.

Instead of showing a static map, the platform continuously fuses ward boundaries, road segment risk, lighting status, incidents, and travel context to generate practical guidance.

Real-Time Flow

1. Data Ingestion

Ward boundaries, road network, incidents, and streetlight datasets are loaded and aligned by geography.

2. Risk Materialization

Each area gets derived metrics (crime index, faulty-light ratio, road risk, accident signals) and a normalized risk profile.

3. Route Intelligence

Route analysis ranks alternatives by safety, time, and cost with explainable trade-offs.

4. Interactive Exploration

Layered map views (risk, crime, lights, combined, roads) reveal why certain areas are flagged.

5. Feedback Loop

Citizen reports and background refresh keep operational data current for decision support.

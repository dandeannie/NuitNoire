About Nuit Noire

Empowering safer nighttime travel across Mumbai through data science and machine learning.

Our Mission

Nuit Noire ("Dark Night") is an AI-powered nighttime safety platform built for Mumbai, Maharashtra. We combine street lighting data, traffic patterns, and historical accident records to map risk zones and suggest safer travel routes.

From Marine Drive to Thane West, we cover 20 neighborhoods — giving citizens the intelligence they need to navigate the city after dark.

How It Works

Data Aggregation

Three datasets — accidents, lighting, traffic — collected for Mumbai neighborhoods

Model Training

Random Forest classifier trained on 600 samples with 5 features (99% accuracy)

Risk Classification

Each zone scored 0–100 and classified as Low, Medium, or High risk

Route Analysis

AI analyzes paths between points and suggests safer alternatives

Citizen Reporting

Community-driven incident reports feed back into the safety ecosystem
